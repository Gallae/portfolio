Phelaia Nemetsk, a former member of the nobility of Bael Turath, she fell into a mysterious slumber when venturing outside the city's walls and woke up hundreds of years later in a tower she didn't recognize. She is still searching for the answer to the question of what magic she fell under and how it kept her alive and young for as long as it did.
