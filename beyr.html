Beyr Ux is a secondary character in the same campaign as Phelaia; as a matter of fact, he considers Phelaia to be his mother. She found the dragon egg that contained him and kept it safe until he hatched out of it, then kept him alive long enough to see him grow from a hatchling to a young dragon. He now fights to protect her whenever and wherever he can.
